Why industrial sponsorship is important?

Industrial sponsors help bridge the gap between academic research and real-world applications. Robotics research often thrives on collaboration with companies that provide funding, technical challenges, and deployment opportunities. As a sponsor, you ensure that academic innovations align with industry needs, accelerating the transition from theory to practical solutions.

Conferences like CROS require funding for venue costs, speaker invitations, workshops, and outreach initiatives. Sponsorships help sustain and expand the event, allowing for a richer experience with high-profile speakers, hands-on demonstrations, and networking sessions that benefit all attendees.

By sponsoring CROS, companies gain access to cutting-edge Brazilian robotics research and emerging talent. This fosters an ecosystem where companies can identify potential hires, form research collaborations, and gain early exposure to disruptive technologies that can give them a competitive advantage. Sponsoring companies get a platform to present their robotics solutions, from automation systems to AI-driven robotics, enhancing their visibility among researchers, students, and fellow industry players. The sponsorship can increase brand recognition, business opportunities, and strategic partnerships.

Brazil's robotics field is growing, but sustained development requires strong support from industry leaders. By sponsoring CROS and SBRobotica, companies contribute to building a robust robotics ecosystem, fostering innovation, and positioning Brazil as a key player in global robotics research and development.

Industrial sponsorship is not just beneficial but essential for the success of CROS and SBRobotica. It ensures financial viability, drives research-industry collaboration, attracts top talent, and strengthens our local Brazilian robotics community.
