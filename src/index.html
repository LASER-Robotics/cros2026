ABOUT EVENT

Welcome to the Second Conference on Robotics from SBRobotica

The 2nd Conference on Robotics (CROS) is a forum the Brazilian Robotics Society (SBRobotica) promotes. It aims to promote a scientific meeting in the field of robotics.

The event will feature plenary talks from recognized experts in the field of robotics and technical sessions with oral and poster presentations of full papers that have been submitted and accepted for the conference.

This sophomore edition of CROS will take place in the beautiful city of João Pessoa, Paraíba, Brazil, from April 27th to 30th, 2026.

  • Abstract and Poster presentations.
  • Plenary talks.
  • Workshops in Robotics, AI and Computer Vision.
